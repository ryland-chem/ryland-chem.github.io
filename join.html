Join the group

We welcome applications from students seeking both undergraduate research experience and graduate research (MSc and PhD). We also welome applications from postdocs who have a well defined independent research project they wish to undertake at UVic.

Interested graduate students are advised to apply to the gradaute program at UVic and be accepted before joining the group, more information regarding requirements and prerequisites can be found here.

Note: All interested graduate students MUST complete the form below. Emails directly to Dr. Giebelhaus will be ignored unless this form is completed, no exceptions.

  • Post Docs: Please contact me via email (rgiebelhaus@uvic.ca) with a one page proposal detailing a research project you wish to undertake in our laboratory at UVic.
  • Graduate students: Please fill out the form located here. To ensure a fair application process*, only applications received through this form will be considered. You are welcome to reach out via email with any questions you may have.
  • Undergraduate students: Please contact me via email rgiebelhaus@uvic.ca to discuss current research opportunities for undergraduates.

    • *This form is meant to remove bias which arise from differences in CV format between countries, by requiring candidates to paste their information into the form. Dr. Giebelhaus receives 100s of applications annually through email which is extremely difficult to manage, therefore only responses to this form are considered. If you do not use this form and instead send an email directly to Dr. Giebelhaus with your CV, it will not receive a response for these reasons.
