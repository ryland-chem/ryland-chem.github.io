About The Giebelhaus Laboratory

The Giebelhaus Laboratory is led by Dr. Ryland Giebelhaus, an Assistant Professor of Chemistry at the University of Victoria (UVic). Our lab is interested in using multidimensional separations, namely comprehensive two-dimensional liquid chromatography (LC×LC-MS) for untargeted metabolomics. We are interested in developing enabling data tools for LC×LC-MS and in applying LC×LC-MS to answer a number of biological questions. Currently, we are interested in pesticide and herbicide exposure in humans and ecosystems, and exploring adulteration in natural products and foods. Visit research to learn more about our research.
