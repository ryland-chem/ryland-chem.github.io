Research Interests and Projects

We specalize in analytical chemistry, particularly separations (chromatography), metabolomics, and chemometrics. We focus on (1) developing and applying comprehensive multidimensional liquid chromatography for use in metabolomics studies, specifically to explore plant chemistry and environment exposures in wildlife, and (2) developing and deploying chemometric tools for processing and interperting data from metabolomics data.
